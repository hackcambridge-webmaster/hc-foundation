What is Hack Cambridge?

Hack Cambridge is the University of Cambridge’s very own hackathon. For 24 intense hours 400 of the most talented students from around the world will push the limits of technology.

Have an awesome idea you’ve been meaning to build? This is your chance.

Keen on learning all about a new piece of technology? Hack Cambridge is the place to be.

We bring the wifi, unlimited coffee, and mentors from some of the world’s most influential companies. You bring a laptop, your creativity, and a desire to make a difference in a big way.

The sky is the limit.

24 hours in the Cambridge Corn Exchange

You'll be creating solutions for the future surrounded by inspirations from the past.
